## 🧾 Support Concierge – CLF-C02 Scope 💼📞

**Support Concierge** is part of  
AWS Support Plans.

It helps customers with **billing and account-related questions**.

---

## 🎯 What Support Concierge Handles

✔ Billing issues  
✔ Invoice questions  
✔ Payment problems  
✔ Account assistance

❌ Does NOT handle technical troubleshooting  
(That’s handled by AWS Technical Support.)

---

## When Is It Available?

Support Concierge is available under:

- **Business Support Plan**
    
- **Enterprise Support Plan**
    

(Not included in Basic plan.)

---

## Example (Exam Scenario)

If question says:

“Company needs help understanding their AWS invoice.”

Correct answer → **Support Concierge**

If question says:

“Application is down and needs technical troubleshooting.”

Correct answer → **Technical Support (Business/Enterprise plan)**

---

## 🔥 Support Concierge vs Technical Support

|Feature|Support Concierge|Technical Support|
|---|---|---|
|Billing help|Yes|No|
|Invoice questions|Yes|No|
|Troubleshoot EC2 issue|No|Yes|

---
