## Operational Expenditure (OpEx) Model – CLF-C02 Scope 💰☁️

The **Operational Expenditure (OpEx) model** means paying for resources **as you use them**, instead of buying infrastructure upfront.

This is a core financial benefit of cloud computing in  
Amazon Web Services.

---

## Simple Meaning

- No large upfront investment
    
- Pay monthly or per usage
    
- Treat cloud cost as operating expense
    

---

## OpEx vs CapEx (Exam Favorite)

|Model|Meaning|Example|
|---|---|---|
|CapEx (Capital Expenditure)|Buy hardware upfront|Purchasing physical servers|
|OpEx (Operational Expenditure)|Pay for usage|Using EC2 on-demand|

---

## Why OpEx Is Important

✔ No need to buy servers  
✔ Lower financial risk  
✔ Flexible budgeting  
✔ Scale costs with business growth

---

## Example (CLF Scenario)

Instead of spending ₹10 lakhs to buy servers:

You launch EC2 instances and pay only for the hours used.

That is OpEx.
