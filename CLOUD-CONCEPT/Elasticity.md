## Elasticity – CLF-C02 Scope ⚡

**Elasticity** is the ability of a system to **automatically increase and decrease resources quickly based on real-time demand**.

It is a key benefit of cloud computing in  
Amazon Web Services.

---

## Simple Meaning

- Demand increases → Resources increase automatically
    
- Demand decreases → Resources decrease automatically
    

You pay only for what you use.

---

## Which AWS Service Enables Elasticity?

- Amazon EC2 Auto Scaling
    

It automatically launches or terminates EC2 instances based on traffic.

---

## Example (Exam Scenario)

During a festival sale:

- Traffic spikes
    
- Auto Scaling launches more instances
    

After sale ends:

- Traffic drops
    
- Extra instances are removed
    

This automatic adjustment = Elasticity.

---

## Elasticity vs Scalability (Exam Favorite)

|Concept|Meaning|
|---|---|
|Scalability|Ability to increase capacity|
|Elasticity|Automatic increase and decrease based on demand|

---

## Key Exam Keywords

- “Automatically adjust capacity”
    
- “Real-time workload changes”
    
- “Scale up and down”
