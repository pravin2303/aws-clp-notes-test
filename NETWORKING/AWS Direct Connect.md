## AWS Direct Connect – CLF-C02 Scope 🔌🌐

**AWS Direct Connect** is a service that provides a **dedicated private network connection** from your on-premises data center to AWS.

Instead of using the public internet, you connect directly to AWS.

---

## Why Use Direct Connect?

✔ More consistent network performance  
✔ Lower latency  
✔ Increased security  
✔ Reduced data transfer costs (in some cases)

---

## How It Works (Simple Concept)

1️⃣ Your company data center  
2️⃣ Dedicated connection to AWS  
3️⃣ Access AWS services privately

Traffic does NOT go over the public internet.

---

## When to Use (Exam Concept)

- Large enterprises
    
- Hybrid cloud environments
    
- High data transfer workloads
    
- Strict security requirements
    

---

## Direct Connect vs VPN (Exam Favorite)

|Feature|Direct Connect|VPN|
|---|---|---|
|Connection Type|Dedicated private line|Encrypted internet connection|
|Performance|More consistent|Depends on internet|
|Cost|Higher|Lower|

---

## Key Exam Keywords 🎯

- “Dedicated private connection” → Direct Connect
    
- “Bypass public internet” → Direct Connect
    
- “Hybrid connectivity” → Direct Connect
