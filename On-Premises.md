## 🏢 On-Premises (On-Prem) – CLF-C02 Concept

**On-premises** means **IT infrastructure that is physically located and managed within an organization’s own building or data center**, instead of using cloud services.

The organization owns and maintains the hardware.

---

## What Exists in On-Premises Infrastructure

Typical components include:

- Physical servers
    
- Storage systems
    
- Networking equipment
    
- Databases
    
- Data center facilities
    

All of these are **managed by the company itself**.

---

## Example

A company runs:

- A physical server in its office
    
- Local database servers
    
- Internal networking equipment
    

This setup is **on-premises infrastructure**.

If the company moves these systems to the cloud using  
Amazon Web Services, it becomes **cloud infrastructure**.

---

## On-Premises vs Cloud

|Feature|On-Premises|Cloud|
|---|---|---|
|Infrastructure location|Company data center|AWS data centers|
|Hardware ownership|Company|Cloud provider|
|Maintenance|Company responsibility|AWS responsibility|
|Cost model|Upfront investment (CapEx)|Pay-as-you-go (OpEx)|

---

## Hybrid Cloud

Sometimes companies use **both on-premises and cloud together**, called **hybrid cloud**.

They connect them using services like  
AWS Direct Connect or VPN.

---
