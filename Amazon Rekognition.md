## 🧠👁️ Amazon Rekognition – CLF-C02 Scope

**Amazon Rekognition** is a service that uses **machine learning** to **analyze images and videos**.

It can automatically identify objects, people, text, and activities.

---

# 🎯 What Amazon Rekognition Can Do

✔ Detect objects in images  
✔ Recognize faces  
✔ Identify celebrities  
✔ Detect unsafe or inappropriate content  
✔ Analyze videos for activities

---

# Example

A security system stores video footage in  
Amazon S3.

Using Rekognition, the system can:

- Detect faces in the video
    
- Identify people
    
- Track suspicious activity
    

---

# Rekognition vs Polly vs Lex (AI Services)

|Service|Purpose|
|---|---|
|Rekognition|Image & video analysis|
|Amazon Polly|Convert text to speech|
|Amazon Lex|Build chatbots|

👉 “Detect faces in images” → Rekognition  
👉 “Convert text to voice” → Polly  
👉 “Create chatbot” → Lex
